 Sales Performance Dashboard

## Overview

This project is an interactive Power BI dashboard developed to analyze sales performance, identify trends, compare regional sales, and monitor key business KPIs.

---

## Features

- Total Revenue
- Total Profit
- Total Orders
- Quantity Sold
- Average Delivery
- Monthly Sales Trend
- Quarterly Sales & Profit
- Sales by Region
- Sales by Category
- Top Selling Products
- Least Selling Products
- Sales by Payment Mode
- Sales by Customer Segment

---

- Microsoft Power BI
- Power Query
- DAX
